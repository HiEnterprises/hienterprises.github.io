Our Vision

HYPED is a pioneering brand dedicated to elevating your everyday. From gourmet energy infusions to sophisticated dining and an exhilarating nightlife, we craft experiences that invigorate body and soul. Every exquisite product is meticulously prepared at our state-of-the-art Gigafactory in the heart of the West Midlands. Current joint executive officers of HYPED are AJ and SJ.

The Daytime Sanctuary

By day, our cafe offers a serene yet stimulating environment. Indulge in artisan energy drinks, premium coffees, and delectable meals designed to fuel your ambition.
