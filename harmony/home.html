Latest Updates

Harmony is constantly evolving! Here's a glimpse of our most recent improvements:

Major UI Overhaul & Enhanced Navigation

This release focuses on significantly improving the user interface and experience across the app, introducing new customization options and polishing existing designs.

  • check_circle_outline **Dashboard Overhaul:** A major redesign of the home screen, now called "Dashboard," with **customizable and reorderable widgets**.
  • check_circle_outline **Streamlined Top Navigation:** The traditional **top toolbar (`AppBar`) has been removed** from the main app scaffold. It's replaced by an **interactive Floating Action Button (FAB)** in the bottom right corner, which expands to reveal four smaller, dedicated menu buttons.
  • check_circle_outline **Polished Designs:** Improved visual layouts for the **Restaurant page** and the **Digital Room Key**, offering a more premium feel.
  • check_circle_outline **Persistent Preferences:** Your widget order, notes, and quick links are now **saved across app sessions**.
